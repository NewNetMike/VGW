Detailed item info

Don Cheadle co-wrote, directed, and stars in this biopic of legendary jazz musician Miles Davis, which takes a free-form approach to its subject’s life that mirrors his playing style. As the story skips around in time, it depicts Davis’ attempts to get his career back on track following a period of listlessness and drug addiction in the ’70s, his relationship with a journalist (Ewan McGregor) who wants to profile him, and his troubled marriage to a former dancer (Emayatzy Corinealdi). Michael Stuhlbarg co-stars.
